Description :

The University of Science and Technology Houari Boumediene (USTHB) was founded in 1974 by the Ministry of Education, officially opened on April 25th, 1974, and was initially named the University of Science and Technology of Algiers (USTA). Later, in 1980, it was renamed in honor of the late President Houari Boumediene. Designed by Oscar Niemeyer, a renowned Brazilian architect, the university became a symbol of Algeria's commitment to scientific and technological advancement. USTHB was the largest university in Algeria until 2013, with over 20,000 students.
